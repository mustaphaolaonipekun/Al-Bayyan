ABOUT US

Discover our School

Al-Bayyan Schools was founded by Al-BayyanFoundation. The school commenced with 10 students in Nusery/Primary School in August 6th, 2013. The school was founded to compliment previous efforts at providing balanced education to students in the nusery/primary sections and to provide a academic programmes in an Islamic environment, preparing students for post-primary school education while inculcating Islamic values in them.

Our Misson

The mission of Al bayyan is to provide an excellent academic challenging, undipsuted morals and islamic education programmes in an Islamic and serene environment, preparing students for post-nusery/primary school education while inculcating Islamic, moral and lofty values in their daily life activities, inline with the hadith and sunnah of the prophet.

Our Vision

Our vision is to instill more Islamic and lofty values in our students and guide them to become model citizens with academic excellence and islamic values which makes them succeed in this life and the hereafter. also provide our children conducive environment which will enable them to achieve their highest potential founded on Islamic ethics.

Why Al Bayyan

Al Bayyan has over 100 Certified Teachers and more than 1000 students passing to Universities, polytechnics and college of education, with good and outstanding results We have been reputabely known over the years for our distinct and excellence in moral, education and islamic affairs all over the country.
