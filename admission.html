ADMISSION

Admission Procedure

All prospective students must first complete an application form, made available in June of the previous year up to the examination day which is usually come in August. Application forms can be obtained from the Account's office by visiting the schools in person, The completed application form, and other documents specified by the school should be returned to the school within one week. Each application is for one prospective student only. After Filling the Entrance Examination Application Form, you will be given an Examination Card where you will see your candidate number. After receiving the completed application form, our admissions committee will review the application in accordance with our admissions criteria. We hope parents would understand this stance of the school and co-operate with us to produce a hardworking and dedicated student for the next phase of their lives. The admissions criteria include: Applicants into JS1 must have turned 9 years old by August of the entry year and must be mature enough to face their studies squarely Result of the Mathematics, English language and Aptitude test must be satisfactory Previous school records are taken cognizance of Parents and their child or ward may or may not be invited for an interview and placement assessment based on the outcome of the exam result. If you are qualified, families will be given an admission letter where a deadline is indicated concerning the first payment of the school fee. After claiming the teller for the registration, with an acceptance of offer letter, then resumption date will be communicated to families.

Admission Forms

Our admission forms are usually made available starting in the month of JUNE. Application forms can be obtained from the Account's office by visiting the schools in person, Exam questions cover elementary English language, Mathematics, and Aptitude. Result of the entrance examination will be released after the examination. The result can be obtained on our notice boards in our schools. Qualified candidates will be issued admission letter.
